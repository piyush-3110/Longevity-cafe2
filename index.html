Our Origin

From the Himalayas

High in the Nepalese mountains, where thin air and ancient soil converge, our coffee grows slowly — developing depth, nuance, and a clarity that lowland beans cannot replicate.

Each harvest is hand-picked at peak ripeness, sun-dried on elevated terraces, and roasted with restraint to preserve the terroir. This is coffee with altitude in every cup.
